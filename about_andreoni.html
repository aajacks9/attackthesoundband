Andre “Oni” Duval

Bass Guitarist/Musical Director/Resident Black Samurai

February 2017

I act as both the bassist and the Musical Director. As the Musical Director I score all the music we compose as well as direct the band during rehearsals and performances to make certain that musically everything runs smoothly. I ended up in the band through Paris J’mal, whom I had been playing with in church for a bit, as well as some other acts in town. After proving my skills to him, he started recommending me to David, and after a couple of auditions, and proving my dedication, I was in. I’ve been playing music since I was in middle school, but I didn’t begin taking it seriously until 2014 when I was about 24 years old. I started playing music as my main career in February of 2017, only a few weeks before I joined Attack The Sound. Besides Attack the Sound, I also play for Chicago Emcee, Tony Famous, am a recording artist for the web series, Sessions with Tandaleria, and have my own FreeJazz/Progressive music solo project known as Rising Deity. Outside of music I’m also a freelance composer, activist, a martial artist, and one of the biggest Nerds you’ll ever meet. I love Comics, video games, anime and martial arts films.
