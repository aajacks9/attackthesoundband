Obywan

Saxophone/ Background Vocals

Joined June 2017

Within Attack the Sound,️ I play the saxophone and sing. Occasionally dabbling in the realm of arrangement, I have assisted in the writing-composition process for the band, specifically the horn section. While singing background vocals for a performance in December of 2015, I was introduced to the Bands leader, David. Immediately, the we became musically bonded over the undeniable talent emerging between us. Over the next few months, we stayed in contact and exchanged music ideas. In May of 2017, the offer was extended to me to join Attack the Sound.

Born to a musical family, I have been singing for over 20 years, playing the saxophone and other woodwind instruments for 15+ and piano for 7. Outside of Attack the Sound, ️ I have a band known as Oby & The Night Cap, and is a part of the music collective Bogus Genius.

Although I'm a pharmacy technician and educator by day, musician by night; I have a B.S. degree in Music-SRT (Sound Recording Technology) from the University of Arkansas at Pine Bluff.
