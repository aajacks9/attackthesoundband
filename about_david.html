Sergeant Dav-O Sounds

Lead Vocalist/Guitarist

Founder of 7 years

I grew up in a musical family singing in the Sunday choir until I found my love was for creating and performing music. I started writing poems and such in 1998 and in 2002 I started learning how to play bass guitar. Around that same time I found my grandfather’s old acoustic and taught myself to play, giving birth to the poems I kept hidden away. Years later my passions would lead me to start a band and over the course of 4 years would develop into Attack the Sound. When I am not rocking the stage at sold out shows I (enjoy long walks on the beach and puppies) I coach gymnastics for the Chicago Park District and serve as the first line defense as an Army Reservist. I enjoy the duality of my life and plan to continue coaching as I climb the latters of success so that my students will always continue to chase their dreams. I’d rather be happy doing what I love; rather than miserable doing something that is comfortable.
