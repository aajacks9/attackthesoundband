Zxxxk The Freak

Lead Guitarist

Joined August 2016

After being inspired by legends like Prince, Jimi Hendrix, Eric Clapton and Steve Vai he began to teach himself to play guitar at the age of 15. With a year of practice he started playing on subways, open mics, and small venues showing off his new found talent and love. Later he joined Attack the Sound and continued with them playing bigger gigs while working on developing himself as a musician.
