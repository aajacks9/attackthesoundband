Jovia Armstrong

Manager

Joined December 2017

From Detroit, MI hails percussionist, composer and 2011 3Arts Teaching Artist Awardee JoVia Armstrong. In 2009, she was nominated for a Detroit Music Award for Outstanding World Instrumentalist. She released an electrifying debut album, "Fuzzy Blue Robe Chronicles" that same year. She is a member of Musique Noire, a "World/Jazz" group consisting of percussion and string instruments. She is a regular lesson (video) contributor for TomTom Magazine. She has had the pleasure of sharing the stage with Amp Fiddler, Trey Anastacio and Michael Franti & Spearhead. She has also performed with artists such as El DeBarge, Martha Reeves, British recording artist Omar, Chilean artist Joe VasConcellos, RES, Frank McComb, Maysa, Rahsaan Patterson, Eric Robeson, Gordon Chambers, flautist Nicole Mitchell, and more. She is currently writing music for Musique Noire's second album and other artists. Check out my website.
