Jovia Armstrong

Manager

Joined December 2017

From Detroit, MI hails percussionist, composer and 2011 3Arts Teaching Artist Awardee JoVia Armstrong. In 2009, she was nominated for a Detroit Music Award for Outstanding World Instrumentalist. She released an electrifying debut album, "Fuzzy Blue Robe Chronicles" that same year. After crossing paths multiple times with David and Paris through the music scene in Chicago, and mentoring both through their own sounds, she joined the band near the end of 2017 as their manager. With the skills she had built being active in the industry she hopes to help take Attack the Sound to the next level. Check out my website.
