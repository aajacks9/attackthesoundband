Paris J’Mal Lawson

Drummer & Percussion

Joined July 2016

Before joining Attack The Sound, he was a freelance audio engineer, producer, and full time salesman at Guitar Center. He got a Facebook message from their now producer and rhythm guitarist, Jesse Parks, that “his band was looking for a drummer”. After years of not playing with any other bands, he was nervous to play with other musicians. The Attack the Sound (ATS) family welcomed him in and helped groomed him into the musician he is now. As time went on and the band started to progress, he began handling the booking and management along with their lead singer David. Outside of the band he is a father of two, loves to cook, and engage in spiritual practices in his free time.
