Our Story

Attack the Sound is a self titled "Chicago Pop" (Chi-Pop) band fusing all the best elements of music into one sound. Founded in 2011, by singer/songwriter and Army vet, David Woulard, the band houses some of Chicago's best musicians and talents as they create a lane of their own. With the use of the base elements of Hip Hop, Jazz, RnB, Soul, and Pop, Attack the Sound is a creative force of ingenuity, class, and style.

Throughout the years the musicians have changed but in April 2016 Attack the Sound finally had the dedicated members to lay the foundation for their success. Using the summer to play as many shows as possible, mostly in the hip hop scene their progress and presence in Chicago has been ever growing.
