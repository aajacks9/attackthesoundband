Our Story

Every so often people will decide to allow themselves to be boxed into a social group, agenda, or sound. This never really clicked for Attack the Sound. Choosing to throw the best parts of creative music into a super blender, with the influences of Windy Cityâ€™s Jazz, Blues, Hip Hop, RnB, and Gospel, Attack the Sound services up a hearty dose of what they call Chi-Pop.

Created in 2010 when singer/songwriter, David Woulard, decided to breath life to his personal songbooks. Throughout the years the musicians have changed, but in April 2016 Attack the Sound decided to lay the foundation for success. Using the summer to play as many shows as possible, mostly in the hip hop scene their progress and presence in Chicago has been ever growing.
