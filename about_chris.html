Chris Coleman

Trombonist/ Background Vocalist

Joined July 2017

Originally, he was invited to see the band play at Simone’s in August by their Saxophonist, Oby, his frat brother (Delta Omicron Omega Inc.) At that show he was introduced to several of the members and started following the band’s Facebook page. He had a feeling that the opportunity would arise to become a part of the horn section, and about a month or so later it did when Oby invited him to a rehearsal as a sort of audition to become a member of the band. A few nights later he was a member playing at the House Party Jam Session that was hosted by the band's leaders, David and Paris. The energy, opportunities, and connections have all been very dope so far and he has been extremely grateful to have been given the chance to become a part of this family.

Outside of the band, he has been doing music for about 12 years, starting with saxophone as his first instrument in high school and gradually learning other instruments throughout high school and college. He attended Jackson State University at which he was a member of the tuba section of the marching band, the concert wind ensemble, the brass ensemble, the vocal jazz ensemble, and the university choir. Currently he is finishing his undergraduate education at Chicago State University where he has founded the CSU Hip-Hop Society. Besides being a trombonist and vocalist, he also produces, composes and arranges music on his own and he hopes to very soon contribute some of his own charts to the band’s repertoire.
