Chris Coleman

Trombonist/ Background Vocalist

Joined July 2017

Originally, I was invited to see the band play at Simone’s in August by Oby, the Saxophonist of the band and my frat brother (Delta Omicron Omega Inc.) At that show I was introduced to several of the members and started following the band’s Facebook page. I had a feeling that the opportunity would arise for me to become a part of the horn section and about a month or so later it did when Oby invited me to a rehearsal as a sort of audition to become a member of the band. A few nights later I was a member of the band playing at the House Party Jam Session that was hosted by our leaders David and Paris. The energy, opportunities, and connections have all been very dope so far and I am extremely grateful to have been given the chance to become a part of this family.

Besides being a trombonist and vocalist, I also produce, compose and arrange music on my own and I hope to very soon contribute some of my own charts to the band’s repertoire. Outside of the band, I have been doing music for about 12 years, starting with saxophone as my first instrument in high school and gradually learning other instruments throughout high school and college. I attended Jackson State University at which I was a member of the tuba section of the marching band, the concert wind ensemble, the brass ensemble, the vocal jazz ensemble, and the university choir. Currently I am finishing my undergraduate education at Chicago State University where I have founded the CSU Hip-Hop society. Other than my musical ventures with Attack The Sound I am developing my own brand as an independent hip-hop/neo-soul artist/producer under the moniker HalisiSoul with my first singles dropping on December 15th of 2017. I am also a part of Planet Saturn, a small collective of creative individuals throughout the city aiming to use their talents as a catalyst for social change and the spreading of good (outer worldly) vibes.
